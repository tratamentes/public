We respect the privacy of our website visitors and are committed to protecting their personal information. This privacy policy outlines the types of personal information that we collect, how we use it, and the steps that we take to ensure that it remains secure.

Information that we collect#

When you visit our website, we may collect certain types of information from you, including:

  • Your name and contact information, such as your name, email address and phone number
  • Information about your browsing behavior on our website, such as the pages that you visit and the links that you click on
  • Demographic information, such as your age and gender

How we use your information#

We use the information that we collect from you in a number of ways, including:

  • To respond to your inquiries and provide you with information about our products and services
  • To improve our website and the user experience for our visitors
  • To send you marketing communications, such as newsletters and promotional offers
  • To comply with legal and regulatory requirements

Sharing your information#

We may share your personal information with third parties in certain circumstances, such as:

  • To our service providers who help us operate our website and provide our services to you
  • To law enforcement agencies, regulatory authorities, or other third parties if we believe that disclosure is necessary to comply with a legal obligation or to protect our rights or the rights of others
  • In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of our business or assets

Cookies and similar technologies#

Our website may use cookies and other similar technologies to collect information about your browsing behavior and preferences. These technologies allow us to provide you with a more personalized experience on our website.

Data retention and security#

We will retain your personal information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected, or as required by law. We take appropriate technical and organizational measures to ensure that your personal information is secure and protected from unauthorized access or disclosure.

Your rights#

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal information that we hold about you
  • Request that we correct any inaccuracies in your personal information
  • Request that we delete your personal information, subject to certain exceptions
  • Object to the processing of your personal information for marketing purposes

Contact us#

If you have any questions or concerns about our privacy policy, or if you would like to exercise your rights in relation to your personal information, please contact us via contact form.
