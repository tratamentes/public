Reservations and Availability#

  • How can I book an appointment? You can book an appointment through our online platform available at https://tratamentes.pt/book-online/ or by calling us at (+351) 937 543 312.

  • What are your opening hours? We are open during the week from 8am to 1pm and at weekends from 10am to 10pm.

  • Do you offer same day bookings? We recommend that you book in advance to guarantee your preferred time slot, but we may have availability for same day bookings. Please check our online platform https://tratamentes.pt/book-online/ or call us (+351) 937 543 312 to enquire.

Massage Services#

  • What types of massages do you offer? We offer a variety of massage techniques and treatments, including Therapeutic Massage (for visceral and musculoskeletal pain dysfunctions), Sports Massage (pre and post-workout), Swedish Massage, Reflexology, Chair Massage, Reiki, Deep Tissue Massage, Aromatherapy Massage, and Lymphatic Drainage Massage.

  • Are enhancements such as aromatherapy or hot stones available? Yes, we offer a variety of enhancements to our massages, such as aromatherapy and hot stone massage. Please let us know at the time of booking if you are interested in any additional treatments.

  • What is included in the price of the massage service? The massage service price includes the time spent with the therapist for the selected massage service. Any enhancements or additional treatments will be charged separately.

What should I wear for my massage? We recommend wearing comfortable clothing that allows for easy movement. Towels will be provided for use during the course of your massage.

Cancellation Policy#

  • What is your cancellation policy? We require a minimum of 24 hours notice to cancel or reschedule your appointment. If you cancel or reschedule within 24 hours of your appointment, you may be charged a cancellation fee equal to 50% of the scheduled service fee.

How can I cancel or reschedule my appointment? You may cancel or reschedule your appointment by contacting us as soon as possible by phone, email or through your cancellation link in your email.
